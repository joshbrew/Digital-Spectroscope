PRINT SETTINGS:
- .4mm nozzle is OK, 
- PLA is OK. We prefer [black PLA+](https://www.amazon.com/PLA-Filament-SUNLU-Dimensional-Plus/dp/B08CVG2RVP/ref=sr_1_2_pp?keywords=carbon+fiber+filament&sr=8-2) or [black PETG-CF](https://www.amazon.com/Filament-Dimensional-Accuracy-Printing-Polycarbonate/dp/B075SXS6NP/ref=sr_1_3_pp?keywords=carbon+fiber+filament&sr=8-3)
- 2 walls OK
- 20% infill OK. Material should not be translucent or highly reflective.
- Use Supports on 90deg overhang for headlamp mount. Not needed anywhere else except maybe the lens caps depending on your printer.
